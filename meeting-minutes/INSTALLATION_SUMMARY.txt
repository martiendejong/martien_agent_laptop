AUDIO QUALITY TIP:
------------------
To capture BOTH sides of a video call (you + other participants):

1. Right-click speaker icon → Sound settings
2. More sound settings → Recording tab
3. Right-click empty area → Show Disabled Devices
4. Right-click "Stereo Mix" → Enable
5. Select "Stereo Mix" when starting recording

This records everything your computer plays (your mic + meeting audio).

WHISPER MODELS:
---------------
Choose based on accuracy vs speed:

  Model    Speed      Accuracy   Use Case
  -----    -----      --------   --------
  tiny     Fastest    Basic      Quick tests
  base     Fast       Good       Default (recommended)
  small    Medium     Better     Important meetings
  medium   Slow       Great      Critical meetings
  large    Slowest    Best       Maximum accuracy

Default: base (good balance)

Change model:
  .\stop-and-transcribe.ps1 -Model small

TRANSCRIPTION TIME ESTIMATES:
-----------------------------
For 1 hour meeting:
  - tiny:   ~2 minutes
  - base:   ~5 minutes
  - small:  ~10 minutes
