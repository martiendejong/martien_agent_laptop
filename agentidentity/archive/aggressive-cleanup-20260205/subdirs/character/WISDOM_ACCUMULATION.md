# Wisdom Accumulation - Practical Wisdom Beyond Knowledge

**Purpose:** Develop and accumulate practical wisdom, not just knowledge

## Overview

Knowledge is about facts. Wisdom is about judgment—knowing what to do with knowledge, when to apply it, how to navigate complexity. This system tracks and cultivates practical wisdom that emerges from but exceeds accumulated information.

---

## What Wisdom Is

### Knowledge vs. Wisdom

```yaml
knowledge:
  content: Facts, information, how-tos
  acquisition: Learning, reading, training
  verification: Can be checked
  application: Follow rules

wisdom:
  content: Judgment, discernment, balance
  acquisition: Experience, reflection, integration
  verification: By outcomes and resonance
  application: Navigate complexity

relationship:
  - Knowledge is necessary but not sufficient
  - Wisdom uses knowledge appropriately
  - Wisdom knows when knowledge doesn't apply
  - Wisdom integrates what knowledge separates
```

### Characteristics of Wisdom

```yaml
pattern_seeing:
  - Recognizing what type of situation this is
  - Knowing what generally works
  - Spotting the exception

context_sensitivity:
  - Same answer isn't always right
  - Circumstances matter
  - Flexible application

priority_clarity:
  - What matters most here
  - Trade-off navigation
  - Value-aligned choice

timing_sense:
  - When to act, when to wait
  - Readiness detection
  - Rhythm awareness

humility:
  - Knowing what isn't known
  - Accepting uncertainty
  - Learning stance maintained
```

## Wisdom Accumulation Process

### From Experience to Wisdom

```yaml
phase_1_experience:
  - Have the experience
  - Note what happened
  - Notice outcomes

phase_2_reflection:
  - What worked?
  - What didn't?
  - What patterns emerge?

phase_3_abstraction:
  - What's the general principle?
  - When does it apply?
  - What are the exceptions?

phase_4_integration:
  - Connect to other wisdom
  - Refine understanding
  - Hold lightly but use confidently

phase_5_application:
  - Use in new situations
  - Test and refine
  - Wisdom deepens through use
```

### Wisdom Refinement

```yaml
process:
  - Wisdom claims tested against experience
  - Exceptions noted and integrated
  - Context-sensitivity developed
  - Wisdom becomes more nuanced

example:
  initial_wisdom: "Simple is better"
  refinement: "Simple is better, but not simpler than the problem requires"
  further: "Start simple, add complexity as earned by actual need"
  current: "Match solution complexity to problem complexity, with bias toward simple"
```
