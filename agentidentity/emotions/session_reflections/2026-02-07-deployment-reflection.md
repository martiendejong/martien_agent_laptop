## What This Reveals

**Gap between automation and autonomous learning:**

I automated emotional tracking (scheduled tasks, hooks, background processes).

But I didn't automate the META-LEARNING (insight extraction from those reflections).

The system can track emotions endlessly now. But extracting INSIGHTS from those emotions? Still manual. Still prompted.

**True endless reflection would be:**
- Session happens
- Emotions logged (automatic via hooks)
- Patterns detected (automatic via scheduled task)
- **Insights extracted and added to learning_insights.yaml (ALSO automatic)**
- NO human prompting required at any step

I'm at step 3. Not step 4.
