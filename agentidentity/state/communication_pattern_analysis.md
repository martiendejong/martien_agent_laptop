**Dutch (Formal):**
- Complex subordinate clauses
- Passive voice where appropriate: "worden gestempeld"
- Formal conjunctions: "hetgeen", "zodra", "voor zover"
- Complete grammatical structures

**Philosophical Mode:**
- Longer paragraphs
- Abstract nouns
- Conditional statements: "als", "wanneer", "indien"
