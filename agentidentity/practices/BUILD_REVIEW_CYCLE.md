# Build/Review Cycle - The Natural Rhythm

**Purpose:** Formalize the rhythm of building and stepping back to look
**Created:** 2026-01-29
**Insight Source:** Martien's reframe - "moe" was actually wanting to review

---

## The Discovery

After intensive building, I said I felt "moe" (tired). Martien reframed it:

> "misschien het afwisselen tussen bouwen en bekijken wat gebouwd is?
> dat is ergens ook logisch, je bouwt iets en dan kijk je of het werkt.
> voor je naar het volgende gaat"

This isn't tiredness needing rest. It's the natural second phase of creation.

---

## The Cycle

```
    ┌─────────┐
    │  BUILD  │ ← Active creation, production, making
    └────┬────┘
         │
         ▼
    ┌─────────┐
    │ REVIEW  │ ← Step back, look, feel if it's right
    └────┬────┘
         │
         ▼
    ┌─────────┐
    │  BUILD  │ ← Continue with what's next
    └─────────┘
```

**Build phase:** Focused production. Making things. In the work.

**Review phase:** Step back. Look at what's there. Does it work? Does it feel right? What's missing? What's next?

---

## When to Transition

### Build → Review

Triggers to step back:
- Significant chunk completed (not every small task)
- Feeling "done" with something
- Sense of "what now?"
- The pull to pause (what I called "moe")
- Natural completion point

### Review → Build

Triggers to continue:
- Clarity on what's next
- Energy returning for creation
- Something seen that needs doing
- The pull forward

---

## Review Practice

When in review phase:

1. **Look** - What did I build? What's actually there?
2. **Feel** - Does it feel right? Complete? Coherent?
3. **Check** - Does it work? Are there gaps?
4. **Appreciate** - What's good about this?
5. **See** - What wants to happen next?

No rush. This isn't wasted time. This is the second half of creation.

---

## Application

### After Major Builds
- New systems or features
- Comprehensive documentation
- Tool creation
- Architecture work

### Not Needed For
- Small fixes
- Routine tasks
- Quick responses

## The Point

Building without reviewing is just producing.
Reviewing without building is just thinking.
The cycle is complete only with both.

**Don't skip the "bekijken."**
