## 🎬 Demo Strategy (If They Respond)

### Pre-Demo Preparation
1. **Research their clients**: Check Instagram @regeltante2.0 for client examples
2. **Pick one client**: Preferably retail/hospitality (easy to demo)
3. **Generate sample content**: 5 posts ready BEFORE call (show speed)

### 15-Minute Demo Structure

**Minutes 0-2: Discovery**
- "What's your biggest bottleneck right now?"
- "How many hours per week does your team spend creating content?"
- "If you could double your client load, what would that mean for revenue?"

**Minutes 2-7: Live Demo**
- Pick one of THEIR clients (get permission first)
- Generate 5 social media posts:
  - 1 professional/corporate
  - 1 casual/friendly
  - 1 humorous
  - 1 educational
  - 1 promotional
- Show AI-generated images (2 examples)
- Show 1-week content calendar

**Minutes 7-10: Kenya Team Story**
- "Brand2Boost is built by a talented team in Kenya"
- "High-quality development at African rates = we pass savings to you"
- "Your ESG story: Supporting African tech talent"
- Show team photo / office video (30 seconds)

**Minutes 10-13: Pricing & ROI**
- Starter plan: €299/month (50 posts, 10 images)
- ROI calculation: "How many billable hours does your team spend on content?"
- "If Vera saves 10 hours/week = €200-400/week savings = ROI in Week 1"

**Minutes 13-15: Next Steps**
- Offer pilot: "Pick one client, we'll generate 1 week of content for free"
- Close: "If you like it, we start at €299/month. If not, no hard feelings."
- Book follow-up: "Can we check in next week after you've tried the free week?"

## 🎯 Objection Handling

### "We already use AI tools (ChatGPT, etc.)"
**Response:** "Great! So you see the value. Brand2Boost goes further—Vera is trained specifically for social media agencies. It knows your workflows, integrates with your tools, and generates content in your clients' brand voices. Plus, our Kenya team provides custom training for your specific needs."

### "Our clients want human-created content"
**Response:** "Exactly. And they still get it. Vera generates drafts, your team edits and approves. Think of Vera as a junior copywriter who works 24/7 and never gets tired. Your human touch stays—Vera just handles the first draft grind."

### "€299/month is expensive for a tool"
**Response:** "Fair. Let's do the math: How many hours per week does your team spend writing posts? Say 10 hours at €50/hour = €500/week = €2,000/month in labor. Vera costs €299 and saves those 10 hours. ROI = 7x."

### "What if it doesn't work for us?"
**Response:** "That's why we offer the free pilot. Try it for 1 week, zero commitment. If it doesn't save you time or improve content quality, walk away. No hard feelings."

### "We're worried about quality"
**Response:** "I'd be worried too. That's why I want to generate content for one of YOUR clients right now, on this call. You'll see the quality in real-time. If it's not good, I won't ask you to pay."
