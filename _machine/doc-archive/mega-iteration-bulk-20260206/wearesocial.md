## 🎁 Pilot Program (Enterprise Version)

### 30-Day Embedded Pilot

**Structure:**
- **Team**: 1 We Are Social production team (5-10 people)
- **Client**: 1 enterprise client (Samsung, Netflix, etc.)
- **Goal**: Prove Vera can 5x iteration speed without quality loss
- **Cost**: Free pilot (€0), then enterprise pricing if successful

**Deliverables:**
- Vera integrated into their workflow (API, Slack bot, whatever they need)
- Training session for production team (2 hours)
- Dedicated support channel (Slack/Teams)
- Weekly check-ins + metrics tracking
- Final presentation: time saved, output increased, team feedback

**Success Metrics:**
- ⬇️ Time to produce 20 ad variations: FROM 40 hours → TO 8 hours
- ⬆️ Campaign iterations tested: FROM 10 → TO 50
- ⬆️ Team satisfaction: "Vera reduced grunt work, more time for creative thinking"
- 💰 Cost savings: Calculate hours saved × production team hourly rate

**Requirements from We Are Social:**
- Access to 1 production team
- NDA (mutual)
- Feedback/data sharing during pilot

## 🎯 Objection Handling (Enterprise Version)

### "We're building our own AI tools"
**Response:** "Smart. Most agencies your size are. Question: how long until your internal AI is production-ready? 12 months? 18? Brand2Boost is ready today. We can be your interim solution—or permanent partner if building in-house becomes too expensive."

### "Data privacy / GDPR concerns"
**Response:** "Critical question. We're GDPR compliant, data processed in EU (or US, depending on your preference), zero data retention after processing. Our Kenya team works under strict NDAs. We can do a full security audit as part of pilot. What specific concerns do you have?"

### "This would disrupt our workflow"
**Response:** "That's why we do embedded pilots. We integrate into YOUR existing workflow, not the other way around. Vera becomes a tool in your stack—like Figma or Adobe. If it disrupts more than it helps, we haven't done our job."

### "Procurement / vendor onboarding takes months"
**Response:** "Understood. That's why we start with a free pilot—no procurement needed. Prove value first, then we go through your vendor process. I'm patient. This is a long-term partnership, not a quick sale."

### "What if our clients find out we use AI?"
**Response:** "They won't. White-label means exactly that—your brand, not ours. In fact, you can position it as 'We Are Social proprietary AI technology' if you want. We're invisible. Your creative authority stays intact."
