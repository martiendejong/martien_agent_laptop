## Customization Checklist

Before sending ANY message, customize:

- [ ] **[Name]** - Use their actual first name
- [ ] **[Company]** - Use their company name
- [ ] **[specific achievement]** - Research: growth, awards, new office, acquisition
- [ ] **[specific services/clients]** - Mention what they actually do or who they serve
- [ ] **[their big client]** - If enterprise, name-drop their actual clients
- [ ] **Tone** - Dutch for NL agencies, English for international
- [ ] **Time estimate** - 15 min for small agencies, 20 min for enterprise
- [ ] **Proof elements** - Add case studies, client names, metrics if you have them

## A/B Testing Variations

### Variation A: Problem-First
Start with: "Hoe voorkom je burnout..." or "What takes longest in your workflow?"

### Variation B: Solution-First
Start with: "Ik heb Brand2Boost gebouwd om..." or "We've built an AI platform that..."

### Variation C: Kenya Team Lead
Start with: "Brand2Boost is gebouwd door een wereldklasse team in Kenya..."

**Test:** Send 10 messages with each variation, track response rates, double down on winner.

---

**Pro Tips:**

1. **Personalize first line** - Reference something specific about them (recent post, award, client win)
2. **Keep it short** - LinkedIn messages get cut off after ~300 characters on mobile
3. **One clear CTA** - "Interesse?" or "Would you be open to..." (don't give multiple options)
4. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am CET** - Highest open/response rates
5. **Use voice messages** (LinkedIn feature) - Stand out in inbox, show personality
6. **Profile optimization** - Make sure YOUR LinkedIn profile looks professional before sending
