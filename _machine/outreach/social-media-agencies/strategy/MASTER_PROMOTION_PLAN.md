**Created:** 2026-02-01
**Goal:** Position Brand2Boost (Vera AI) as the go-to AI platform for social media agencies + showcase Kenya team as competitive advantage
**Target Market:** Dutch social media agencies (15-80 employees)

### 1. **AI-Powered Content Production at Scale**
- Generate social media posts, website copy, email campaigns, ad creatives in minutes

### 2. **Kenya Development Team - Competitive Advantage**
- **Cost Efficiency**: High-quality development at African rates (not Dutch rates)

- Agencies can brand Vera as their own AI tool

- API integration for existing workflows

## 📊 Promotion Channels & Tactics

### Phase 1: Direct Outreach (Weeks 1-4)

**LinkedIn Outreach**
- Target: Founders, CEOs, Heads of Production at 15-80 person agencies
- Message: Personal, solution-focused (see templates/)
- Volume: 5 personalized messages per day
- Follow-up: 3-message sequence (Day 0, Day 7, Day 14)

**Email Campaigns**
- Segment 1: Social media specialists (Regeltante type)
- Segment 2: Digital agencies (SocialBrothers type)
- Segment 3: Large production agencies (WeAreSocial type)
- Cadence: 1 email per week for 4 weeks

**Cold Calling** (if LinkedIn/email fails)
- Script: "Hi, I'm Martien from Brand2Boost. I help social media agencies scale content production with AI. Do you have 2 minutes?"
- Qualify: "How many people on your content team? What's your biggest bottleneck?"
- Pitch: "Can I show you how we cut content production time by 70%?"

### Phase 2: Proof of Concept (Weeks 2-8)

**Free Pilot Program**
- Offer: "Pick one client, we'll generate 1 week of content for free"
- Requirement: Feedback session + testimonial if happy
- Goal: 3-5 successful pilots = case studies

**Demo Days**
- Host virtual "AI for Agencies" demo sessions
- 30-minute format: 10min talk, 15min live demo, 5min Q&A
- Record and repurpose as marketing content

**Case Study Production**
- Document: Problem → Solution → Results (quantified)
- Format: PDF (for proposals), blog post (for SEO), video (for social)
- Highlight: Kenya team role in each success story

### Phase 3: Content Marketing (Weeks 4-12)

**Blog Posts (SEO-focused)**
- "How AI is Transforming Social Media Agency Workflows"
- "Why Dutch Agencies Are Partnering with African Tech Teams"
- "The True Cost of Content Production (And How to Cut It 70%)"
- "Case Study: How [Agency] Scaled from 20 to 40 Clients Without Hiring"

**LinkedIn Thought Leadership**
- Post 3x per week: AI trends, agency insights, Kenya team highlights
- Share behind-the-scenes: "Meet our Kenya developers" series
- Engage: Comment on agency posts, join industry discussions

**Video Content**
- "Meet the Team: Inside Our Kenya Development Office"
- "Live Demo: Generate 1 Week of Social Content in 10 Minutes"
- "Client Testimonials: [Agency] on Working with Brand2Boost"

### Phase 4: Partnership & Referrals (Weeks 8+)

**Agency Partner Program**
- Revenue share: 20% recurring commission for referrals
- Co-marketing: Joint webinars, case studies, events
- Exclusive features: Partners get early access to new Vera capabilities

**Industry Events**
- Attend: Dutch marketing/agency conferences
- Speak: Offer to present on "AI in Agency Workflows"
- Booth: Demo station + Kenya team video call

**Referral Incentives**
- Current clients: €500 for every agency they refer
- First 10 agencies: Lifetime 30% discount
