## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Outreach
- ❌ Generic messages (always personalize first line)
- ❌ Too long (keep under 300 characters for LinkedIn)
- ❌ No clear CTA ("Let me know your thoughts" = bad, "15-min demo?" = good)
- ❌ Sending Friday afternoon or Monday morning (low response rates)

### Demo
- ❌ Talking too much (80% listening, 20% talking in first 5 minutes)
- ❌ Generic examples (use THEIR clients/projects)
- ❌ Skipping pain discovery (ask "what's your biggest bottleneck?")
- ❌ No clear next step (always book follow-up or pilot before ending call)

### Pilot
- ❌ Overpromising ("Vera will 100% solve everything" = no, be realistic)
- ❌ No success metrics (define what "success" looks like upfront)
- ❌ Poor onboarding (make it easy for them to use Vera)
- ❌ Disappearing during pilot (check in weekly, be responsive)

## 🎯 Focus Principle

**80/20 Rule:**
- Spend 80% of effort on **Tier 1 agencies** (Regeltante-type, 10-30 people)
- Spend 20% of effort on **Tier 2 agencies** (Social Brothers-type, 30-100 people)
- Spend 0% on **Tier 3 (Enterprise)** until you have 5+ case studies

**Why?**
- Tier 1 = Fast decision cycles (weeks, not months)
- Tier 1 = Clear pain points (capacity bottleneck)
- Tier 1 = Lower expectations (easier to exceed)
- Tier 1 = Better testimonials (personal, enthusiastic)

**Build credibility with Tier 1 → Use case studies to win Tier 2 → Eventually tackle Tier 3 (We Are Social)**
