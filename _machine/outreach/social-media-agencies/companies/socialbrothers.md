**Response:** "Absolutely. Vera generates the first draft—your team edits for client specifics. It's like having a junior copywriter who writes 10x faster. Your quality control stays, Vera removes the blank page problem."

### "We have copywriters on staff"
**Response:** "Great! Vera multiplies their output. Instead of writing from scratch, they edit and refine. 10 hours of writing becomes 2 hours of editing. Same quality, 5x faster."

### "How does it handle technical content?"
